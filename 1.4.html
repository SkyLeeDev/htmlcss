1.4 - Describe the risks that might impact digital projects ?

Digital projects may fail for a number of reasons, firstly as a start up project there will be many obstacles and failures that may occur but it is natural for this to happen, failure is often needed in order to succeed. Learning about your projects strengths and weaknesses can only benefit you in the long term in pushing to rectify said issues in the project.

Additionally, other risks that digital prokects may face include cyber security issues such as hackers and viruses. If your project collects any type of data, there is a possibiltiy of data being stolen, there needs to be extra cautious steps taken in order to ensure the saftey of any personal data.

Black hat hackers are known as people that illegally hack into computer systems and networks with malicious intent often destroying, exploiting and stealing data or even causing your network or website to be shut down. Similarly you can use what is known as a white hat hacker to purposely breach the security of your network and systems in order to learn about the faults in your network's security, helping to protect from any possible black hat hackers.
