2.5 - Review outcomes to make sure they match requirements and are fit for purpose

Whilst creating my website, I constantly saved changes and put them to test within my webrowser to confirm whether or not I was on the right track of what I wanted to achieve.

The content of my website was based on a work document that I wrote, I checked through all of my content for mistakes aswell as just to amend the structure of the paragraphs if needed.

The HTML used for my website was thoroughly checked during and after the creation of my website, to ensure that all links worked as they should whether that be a redirection to an external site like Trello or to another page within my website.

Similarly the CSS was also thoroughly checked in order to comply with my original colour schemes rather than each page being completely different, I kept the base design of my website the same so that it was clear that all pages were from the same website and so that users could easily distinguish that.

Where needed, I included references to external sites that I had used when researching certain topics such as GDPR as I had some knowledge but needed to do a little further reading in order to properly answer the question.
