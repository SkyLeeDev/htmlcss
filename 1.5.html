When selecting and using IT systems and software for a business it is important to research various systems and softwares that can be used to complete any tasks you need however it is also important to re-evaluate any previous IT systems and softwares which may have been implemented beforehand as to make sure the new systems/softwares can rectify any issues you may have had with a previous one and to establish a more updated or organised version of what you require.

It may also be beneficial to give a survey and/or trial of potential softwares to the employees who will be required to use the system and softwares, gaining input from a more user based perspective can greatly increase chances of selecting a more compatible software

Research into similar businesses and their respected system and softwares of choice may also provide a great insight when making a decision for your business, along with taking into account the cost of said softwarese or components needed to put the system in place. The aim being to incorporate the cheapest alternatives whilst avoiding sacrificing the quality of the end product.

A company also needs to take into consideration any hardware (and their respected operating system) required such as PC's, laptops and printers. There will be different benefits of each, it's all dependant on what the company wants to use it for. The security that you have in place will also be important.
